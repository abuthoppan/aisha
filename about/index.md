## What is AISHA?

AISHA stands for **AI for Software Heuristics and Automation**. It's a blog, tutorial platform, and open-source resource hub built for people who design, build, and operate software systems — particularly in startup and lean-team environments.

The goal is simple: make solution architecture accessible, practical, and lean. No enterprise bloat. No 200-page specs that nobody reads. Just the frameworks, decisions, and thinking patterns that actually help you ship better products.

---

## Who is this for?

**Solution Architects** who want frameworks and decision tools to communicate complex solutions clearly — to both technical teams and business stakeholders.

**Startup Founders** who need to understand architecture decisions well enough to have informed conversations with their tech team and make smarter investment choices.

**Developers & Tech Leads** who are stepping into more strategic roles and want to think at the system level, not just the code level.
